Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is used when you visit (https://emmenthal-native.vercel.app/) (the “Site”).

Personal Information

The app runs entirely in your browser, and none of the data accessed through your google account is transferred to our servers. We do not store or collect any information about you or your calendars as part of your use of this app.

Some data is stored in your browser's local storage between sessions. If you don't wish for this data to be stored, then click the "Sign Out" button you are done using the app, and the local storage will be cleared.

The Site uses Google Authentication to verify your identity and to access a limited portion of your Google Calendar data.

When you authenticate with google, the app will have access to:

  1. Your basic personal profile information (name, email, profile picture, etc.){" "}
  2. The names of the calendars you have access to in Google Calendar.
  3. Free/busy information for the calendars you have access to in Google Calendar.

The app WILL NOT have access to information about individual calendar events (such as event titles, descriptions, invitees etc.)

The app WILL NOT have access to any other data associated with your google account.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

The information about (email, name, profile picture) are only used to show the identity of the user signed into the application in the upper right hand corner.

The information about your calendars is used by the app to calculate your free time and to display those times in the app's UI.

None of this information is transmitted to or stored on our servers during your use of the app.

THIRD PARTY ACCESS TO INFORMATION

By signing in with your google ID, you will be giving Google permission to collect information, track your activity and use your information according to their privacy policy which you can find here: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/.

We do not have access to any of the information collected by Google, aside from what it explained above.

CHANGES

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.
